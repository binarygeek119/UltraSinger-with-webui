About UltraSinger WebUI

This WebUI is integrated into the UltraSinger repository: the browser layer queues work, and each job runs UltraSinger.py in a separate subprocess with its own output folder under data/jobs/.

Dependencies match the main project (WhisperX, Demucs, yt-dlp, FFmpeg, etc.). The WebUI adds FastAPI, Uvicorn, and optional pystray for desktop tray mode.

yt-dlp: restricted videos often need a valid cookies.txt. Export cookies from your browser in Netscape format and upload them from the Home page, or set the path in Settings.
